  • Chad B. Hovey, Ph.D.

Experience

Dr. Hovey has given expert testimony in state and federal courts, at deposition and trial.

He is the owner of Hovey Consulting, started in 2004.

He has worked with the 3M Biosciences Laboratory, Exponent Failure Analysis, MSC Software, Piziali & Associates, and Delta V Biomechanics.

Education

  • Ph.D. Mechanical Engineering, Stanford University, 2000
  • M.S. Civil Engineering, Stanford University, 1995
  • B.S. Mechanical Engineering, Montana State University, 1994

For his doctoral thesis, Dr. Hovey created a state-of-the-art computer program to simulate stress and motion of a total hip arthroplasty. The research, done at Stanford University and the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne), was funded by Stanford University, the National Science Foundation, and the Department of Veterans' Affairs.
