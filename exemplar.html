Introduction

Hovey Consulting LLC, an engineering firm dedicated to the science of accident and injury analysis, seeks a person of a specific height and weight as well as a vehicle of a certain year, make, and model to assist in the reconstruction of an accident scenario.

Occupant

We need a person on two dates: (1) the qualifying date and (2) the photo shoot date.

We are seeking a person with the following characteristics:

  • Sex: male
  • Age: 30 to 65 years
  • Height: 5 foot 8 inches tall without shoes (between 5 foot 7.5 inches to 5 foot 8.5 inches is acceptable)
  • Weight: 220 pounds without shoes (215 to 225 pounds is acceptable)

You must be within the stated ranges to be eligible for this study. To qualify, you must visit our office for a brief 10 minute interview wherein we verify your height, weight, and availability. Once you have qualified, we will then schedule you for a photo shoot date.

On the photo shoot date, you must be at our office at the assigned time. While you sit in an exemplar vehicle, we will take photographs of your driving positions. We will take your measurements, including weight, height, and seated height. We may also take other measurements, such as distances between your body and locations within the vehicle. The photographs and measurements may be used as exhibits in a court of law.

Compensation: We do not pay you for the qualifying date. We pay only for the photo shoot date. On the photo shoot date, we pay an hourly rate, for a maximum of two hours. Payment will be made only upon completion of the photo shoot.

Vehicle

We need a vehicle on two dates: (1) the qualifying date and (2) the photo shoot date.

We are seeking a vehicle with the following characteristics:

  • Year: 2003 is preferred, but any year between 1997 and 2004, inclusive, is acceptable
  • Make: Ford
  • Model: F-150 4x2 pickup

Your vehicle must be within the stated ranges to be eligible for this study. To qualify, you must visit our office for a brief 10 minute interview wherein we verify your vehicle characteristics. Once you have qualified, we will then schedule you for a photo shoot date.

On the photo shoot date, you must be at our office at the assigned time. We will place an exemplar occupant in your vehicle while we take photographs and measurements. Your vehicle will not be driven during the study. Your vehicle will not be damaged in any way. The photographs and measurements may be used as exhibits in a court of law.

Compensation: We do not pay you for the qualifying date. We pay only for the photo shoot date. On the photo shoot date, we pay an hourly rate, for a maximum of two hours. Payment will be made only upon completion of the photo shoot.
